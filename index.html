About

The website is about autoimmune diseases, which lead to the immune system attacking healthy cells and tissues in the body, causing inflammation and tissue damage. Targeted treatments are required to inhibit or reduce the activity of autoimmune antibodies, and many monoclonal antibodies have been developed for this purpose......

what is MABS ?

Monoclonal antibodies are biological products that are produced in laboratories using the hybridoma technology, which is a technique used to produce highly specific and sensitive antibodies. These antibodies are designed to target specific receptors in the body and are used for various medical purposes, such as cancer treatment and autoimmune diseases. Monoclonal antibodies are characterized by their high specificity and sensitivity, which make them effective in targeting disease-causing agents in the body while minimizing damage to healthy tissues
